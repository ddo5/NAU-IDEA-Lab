BossLaser HP-3655


The HP-3655 (high power) offers a multi functional laser that engraves and cuts through deep materials - including metal! Capable of cleanly cutting 18 ga Stainless steel (1.02mm). Equipped with 150W CO2 laser tube. 120 psi rated laser head w/ 1/4" high pressure air lines and hardware. Industrial CW-5200 Water Chiller w/ temp alert to protect your laser tube. Vacuum table with tray conveniently captures product. Safety door interlocks and limit switch prevent laser firing with the door open.
